About Justin Bieber

Justin Drew Bieber is a Canadian singer, songwriter, and actor. He was discovered on YouTube and signed to RBMG Records in 2008. He became a teen sensation with his debut album "My World 2.0" and has since released several chart-topping hits.

Justin has won numerous awards, including a Grammy Award, 20 Billboard Music Awards, and 18 MTV Europe Music Awards. He has sold over 150 million records worldwide, making him one of the best-selling music artists of all time.
